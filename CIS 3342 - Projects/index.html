Project 1 - Form Processing

Student Feedback Form

Link

This project is a student feedback form created in HTML to gather insights on courses and instructors. It collects student details such as their name and student ID. The form includes a list of courses, allowing students to provide specific feedback. The survey consists of 20 questions: 12 related to the course and 8 pertaining to the professor. For each question, the user can choose from the options: strongly agree, agree, neutral, disagree, and strongly disagree.
